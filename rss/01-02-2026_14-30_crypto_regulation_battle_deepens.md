The Clarity Act represents foundational legislation that would permanently establish which federal agencies oversee crypto markets, provide lasting legitimacy to the crypto industry within mainstream finance, and determine how deeply traditional banks can integrate crypto services. With implications for 00B in potential bank deposits and the power to cement the US as the 'crypto capital of the planet', this bill would create structural changes to how digital and traditional financial systems coexist and interact for years to come. The outcome will define the regulatory landscape governing crypto assets, DeFi products, and tokenized securities—far-reaching consequences that extend well beyond short-term market movements or temporary legislative delays.
