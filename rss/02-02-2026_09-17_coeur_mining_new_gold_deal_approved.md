This represents a permanent corporate acquisition that fundamentally alters the company structure. New Gold Inc will cease to exist as an independent entity once the deal closes in the first half of 2026. All NGD shares will be permanently converted to Coeur Mining shares at a fixed ratio (0.4959 Coeur shares per NGD share). This creates a lasting combined North American precious metals platform with 38% ownership for former NGD shareholders. The consolidation is a definitive structural change in the mining sector, not a temporary market fluctuation or short-term event. The integration of New Gold's assets (Rainy River and New Afton) into Coeur Mining will have enduring operational and market impacts.
