2. **Leverage**: ~3x debt/EBITDA with $350-360M annual interest expense creates financial rigidity.

3. **Known Product Risks**: Vertex arbitration and Alyftrek conversion uncertainty, though management remains positive.

- **Economic Moat**: POSITIVE - Internalization permanently improves cost structure and governance alignment; synthetic royalties expand competitive capabilities

- **Value vs Price**: MIXED - Strong fundamentals and cost savings (signal) but growth slowdown may disappoint near-term expectations (noise)

The 2026 guidance appears conservative given the cost leverage and pipeline potential. Long-term investors should view this as confirmation of structural improvement rather than merely missing expectations, though they should monitor the growth trajectory and execution on pipeline catalysts.
