Summary: Spain-based Grenergy closed $355M senior non-recourse financing led by BNP Paribas (with Santander and Rabobank) to fund its Central Oasis platform in Chile, comprising 398MW solar capacity and 1.4GWh storage across Gran Teno, Tamango, and Planchón hybrid plants. The projects are backed by long-term PPAs and will be operational between 2026-2027.
