# Crypto Signal Analyzer - Persistent Memory

## Analysis Framework Version 1.0
- **Created**: 2026-02-23
- **Focus Assets**: BTC, ETH, BNB, SOL, XRP, USDC, USDT, stablecoins
- **Analysis Depth**: Long-term signals vs short-term noise

## Confidence Score Weights (Validated)
1. Source Reliability: 30% - Tier 1 sources carry 90-100% weight
2. News Clarity & Specificity: 25% - Quantifiable impact is critical
3. Directness of Impact: 25% - Direct operational effects weighted highest
4. Conflict & Consensus: 20% - Consensus adds value, debate reduces confidence

## Key Insights
- Minimum confidence threshold: 88% (enforced)
- Three-filter approach: Economic Moat → Macro/Structural → Value vs. Price
- Economic moat changes are rare but high-impact long-term signals
- Macro/structural changes (regulation, adoption) take 6-18 months to materialize

## Known Challenges
- Crypto news velocity is 3-5x higher than traditional finance
- Retail vs institutional news have different reliability profiles
- Stablecoin news often conflates liquidity issues with solvency issues
- Regulatory news requires jurisdictional weighting (US > EU > Asia > Rest)

## Common False Signals (To Ignore)
- Minor exchange listing announcements
- Social media sentiment spikes
- Influencer endorsements
- Volume spikes without price confirmation
- Liquidation event announcements in bull markets

1. Coindesk (institutional-grade)

4. Reuters (breaking news)
5. CoinMarketCap (data aggregator)
