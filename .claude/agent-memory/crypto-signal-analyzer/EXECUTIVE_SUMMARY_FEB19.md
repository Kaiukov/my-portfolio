## The Confidence Scoring Formula

**Confidence Score = (Source Reliability × 0.30) + (Clarity × 0.25) + (Directness × 0.25) + (Consensus × 0.20)**

### Why This Weighting?
- **Source Reliability (30%)**: Tier 1 sources must anchor all analysis
- **Clarity (25%)**: Vague announcements = interpretable = risky
- **Directness (25%)**: Most news is tangential, not transformative
- **Consensus (20%)**: Single announcements can be spin; multiple sources = validation

### Why No Headlines Scored 88%+?
This is **healthy and expected** because:
- Most daily news originates from secondary sources (aggregators, not primaries)
- Single announcements lack verification from independent sources
- Many announcements are positioning, not binding commitments
- Impact is often delayed or contingent on future events
